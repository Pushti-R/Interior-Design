GENERAL FAQs

Have any doubts ?
Feel free to reach out to us and will solve together.




What if I need additional help with some other odds and ends?

Our designers are happy to perform additional design services outside of the project scope at an hourly rate if you need them. Let us know what you have in mind and we can discuss your options!

How long does the remodeling design process take?

The timeline of our design process varies depending on the scope and size of your project. Following your initial consultation, we will provide a proposal outlining the projected scope of work and a rough estimate of the timeline.

Can you refer us to a good Contractor? Architect? Painter? Etc.?

Absolutely! We have long standing relationships with the professionals who we trust. We’ve got high standards when it comes to keeping our clients happy, so we only work with the best. We don’t take referral kickbacks either; we just think they’re the best in the industry!

When should we enlist the services of a designer?

The earlier the better! We love to help create the initial vision for the space, and the more time we have to get your project scheduled, the better.
